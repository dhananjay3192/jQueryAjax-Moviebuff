Movies are capable of forming the pathway to our emotions. Reading stories can do that too but the visual flow of imagination is much clear and vivid in movies. Depending on your view, whether you look at it through the eyes of an antagonist or a protagonist, you tend to feel the turmoil of actions and emotions. It can let you discover those personal emotions which you dont encounter on a regular basis. Remember those superhero movies in which, after the end, you walk out of the theatre and the superhero's theme music starts playing in your mind making you feel you are the one. Thats's the kind of nostalgia it can cast on you, there are other types too, depends on the type of the movie you were watching. Cheers to this form of expression.

Dhananjay Patania
